About me

I am a third-year PhD student in mathematics at the Institut für Mathematische Stochastik at the University of Münster and at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI MiS) in Leipzig. My advisors are Martin Huesmann and Felix Otto.

My research lies at the intersection of analysis and probability with a focus on the optimal matching problem.

I am supported by the Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft (DFG) through the SPP 2265 and through the Cluster of Excellence Mathematics Münster, and by the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MPI MiS).

I have received both my bachelor's and master's degree in mathematics from Università degli studi di Pisa where I was advised by Dario Trevisan.

You can contact me via email at francesco.mattesini at uni-muenster dot de.
